Hocus Pocus

HOME | CAST | REVIEW

HOME

After moving to Salem, Massachusetts, teenager Max Dennison explores an abandoned and reportedly haunted cottage with his sister Dani and his crush, Allison . After brushing off the story Allison tells him as superstitious 'hocus pocus', Max accidentally frees the evil witches known as the Sanderson Sisters. Now, with the help of a magical talking cat Binx, the kids must steal the witches' book of spells to stop them from stealing the souls of all the children of Salem and becoming immortal.

Genre: Comedy, Family, Fantasy
